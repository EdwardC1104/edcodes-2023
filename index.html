Increment:
Revision Planner

On August 13th 2021, I released my first app - Increment: Revision Planner. Its purpose is to support students by encouraging them to form a routine around their studying. By using the Pomodoro method, the app's study timer promotes a healthy balance of work sessions and breaks. Reminder notifications and streaks help keep students on track. I sold the app to Revisely in March 2022.

Control Panel

Using a Raspberry Pi, I built a small control panel that turns on and off devices around my desk. It is written in Python and communicates with smart plugs and my desktop computer over the network. This gave me experience working with headless IoT devices. The enclosure was designed and printed by Sam Mantell.
